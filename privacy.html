Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

An independent developer operates the Avail mobile application. This page explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, username, and email address. When you use the app, we store your availability schedule, plans you create or join, and messages you send within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the Avail service — matching your availability with friends, enabling plans, and delivering messages. We do not sell your data.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party infrastructure provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab in the app. Deleting your account permanently removes all your data from our servers.

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@availnetwork.com
